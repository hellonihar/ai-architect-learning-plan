# Research Papers Reading List

## Must-Read

| Paper | Topic | Why |
|-------|-------|-----|
| [Attention Is All You Need](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) | Transformer architecture | Foundation of modern LLMs |
| [RAG Survey](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.10997) | Retrieval-Augmented Generation | Comprehensive RAG taxonomy |
| [The Prompt Report](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.06608) | Prompt Engineering | Systematic prompt engineering survey |
| [Tree of Thoughts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.10601) | Reasoning | Deliberate reasoning with LLMs |
| [ReAct](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629) | Agent reasoning | Synergizing reasoning and acting |
